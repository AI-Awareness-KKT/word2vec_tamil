Word Similarity:
This application uses a Word2Vec model trained on large Tamil text data. Each word is represented as a numerical vector in high-dimensional space.

Similarity Score:
When you enter a word, the model computes cosine similarity between the input word and all other words in the vocabulary. The closer the score is to 1.0, the more semantically similar the words are.

Why Similar Words Appear:
Words that occur in similar contexts (for example, places, emotions, actions) tend to have similar vector representations. This allows the model to capture semantic meaning beyond exact spelling.

PCA Visualization:
The model vectors are originally hundreds of dimensions. Principal Component Analysis (PCA) reduces them to 2 dimensions so that relationships between words can be visualized on a 2D graph.

Graph Interpretation:
The input word is shown in red, and related words are shown in blue. Words plotted closer together are more semantically related according to the model.
